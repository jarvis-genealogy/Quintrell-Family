Quintrell family

A record of the Quintrell family


This record is dedicated to the work of Neil Quintrell. Neil's mother Olive was sister to my grandmother Alice.

His family website is the result of many hours of research and dedication. However, sadly, once a website hosting subscription is finished, the site is lost. Fortunately by chance I had made a copy and originally published it using Netlify. I can now include it as part of the genealogy of the Jarvis family in Australia and re-publish it thanks to GitHub.

Lynn Jarvis
