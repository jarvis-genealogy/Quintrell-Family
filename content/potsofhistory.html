This for Charmian, after meeting her in the University of Sydney's museum of antiquities: an anniversary poem

 

One time

I found you crying

among the pots of history

for their beauty

exquisite forms

holding an infinity

of memories

 

One time

these pots were fashioned new

serving the unexceptional duty

of every day

Memories are precious

and are to be kept and held

and viewed with love

but are not love

 

Love is made of hope

which we fashion new

with common clay and imagination

Tomorrow

we may test it in the fire
