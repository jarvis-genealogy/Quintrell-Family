This is a collection of various writings over the past thirty years. It includes religious songs from the period of time I was involved in the Uniting Church. Permission for reproduction of the words of these songs must be sought from Pilgrim Uniting Church, 12 Flinders St, Adelaide, South Australia 5000 who are the holders of the copyright. Copyright for the music for these (and other songs) is held by the individual composers as indicated on each song, and permission for reproduction of their work must be sought directly from them.
