From the sky above, from the earth below

Comes the song of glory to creation's God.

All things created join creation's song:

'How great God's name is in all the earth!'

 

Ev'ry living cell, ev'ry shape and form

From the smallest atom to the largest sun,

Blazes with glory for creation's God.

'How great God's name is in all the earth!'

 

All creation sings of creation's God:

Ev'ry star and planet, ev'ry moon and sun

Tells of the power of creation's God:

'How great God's name is in all the earth,

in all the earth, in all the earth!'
