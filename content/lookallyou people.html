Look, all you people.

Listen, all you lands.

The Christ now is born. The Christ now is born.

 

This is the time

Spoken by the prophets.

The new age is come. The new age is come.

Wonder, all you lands.

For God is among us – ‘Emmanuel’.

 

Promise of sages.

Vision of the ages.

Messiah is come. Messiah is come.

 

Come, all you people.

Join us, all you lands.

With angels sing glory, With angels we sing

Glory in the highest,

And peace on the earth. Peace on the earth.’
