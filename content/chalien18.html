For our eldest daughter on her 18th birthday.

 

In the soft morning dark she woke us

The stillness promised heat

With uncertain courage

we comforted each other

and ventured the unmade road to parenthood

Without knowledge she knew her time

and as dawn leapt in the sky

her cry broke the morning

and sunlight crowned her head with flame
