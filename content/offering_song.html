Move within our giving,

Spirit of renewing.

Breathe into our living

strength enough to serve.

As we bring our offerings,

life and love and laughter,

and our daily tasks

are offered with our gifts.

 

Touch us in our greeting,

Spirit of our healing.

Weave into our being

grace enough to love.

Move within our giving,

Spirit of renewing.

Breathe into our living

strength enough to serve.

 

Within the risk of giving,

hope and hurt and healing

are alike received,

accepted as we give.

Lead us in our leaving,

Spirit of our calling;

Present in our going,

surprise us on our way.
