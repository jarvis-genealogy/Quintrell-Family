The website of the Quintrell family:

Australia and Canada

This website has been created by L Neil Quintrell of Hawthorndene South Australia, and contains: 

Historical and genealogical information from the time of migration of the Quintrell, Scott, Green, Pearce, Datson and Long families to South Australia in the mid nineteenth century;

Family stories including tributes to Neil's parents Olive and Norman Quintrell;

Information about the Canadian Quintrells - descendants of Neil's brother, Robert Quintrell; and

Neil's collected poetry, songs and stories.

Contact Neil for further information.
