I draw the borders of my life clearly

with words carefully chosen

experience thoughtfully labelled

I am secure there

 

But today an experience invaded me

that defies my definitions

and refuses my labels

It possessed me

and words cannot contain it

 

I want to tell you about it

but my words will not respond

What I feel cannot be told in words

 

Thank you for that experience

real but not defined

It lives between us

within us

and defines us
