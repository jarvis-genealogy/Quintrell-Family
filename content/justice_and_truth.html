Justice and truth you have shown us, O God.

Mercy and freedom are your gifts.

Wisdom and beauty are born in your mind.

Science and art shout your praise.

Let all the nations rejoice in your love

And raise their voices in song.

Now all the nations your greatness will sing

And all the people rejoice.

For all your gifts Lord, we praise you.

For all your gifts Lord, we praise.
